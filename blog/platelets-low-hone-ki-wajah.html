Platelets Low Hone Ki Wajah
Causes & Treatment

By Healthcare Clinical Laboratory - Jowar, Buner - Jowar, Buner • Health Guide

🩸 Platelets Kya Hoti Hain?

Platelets (thrombocytes) khoon mein chhote cells hote hain jo khoon jamane (clotting) mein madad karte hain. Jab aapko chot lagti hai toh platelets mil kar khoon rokti hain. Normal platelets count 150,000 se 400,000 per µL hota hai.

Jab platelets 150,000 se kam ho jayein toh isko thrombocytopenia kehte hain. Yeh ek serious condition ho sakti hai.

⚠️ Platelets Kam Hone Ki Wajuhat

  • Dengue Fever: Dengue mein platelets tezi se girte hain — yeh sabse aam wajah hai hamare area mein
  • Viral Infections: Typhoid, Hepatitis, aur doosre viral infections platelets kam kar sakte hain
  • Anemia: Iron deficiency ya vitamin B12 ki kami se bhi platelets affect hoti hain
  • Bone Marrow Problems: Leukemia ya doosri bone marrow diseases
  • Medications: Kuch dawaiyan jaise antibiotics aur painkillers
  • Autoimmune Diseases: ITP (Immune Thrombocytopenic Purpura)
  • Pregnancy: Kuch auraton mein pregnancy ke doran platelets kam ho jati hain
  • Liver Disease: Cirrhosis ya chronic liver problems

🔍 Symptoms — Pehchanein Ke Platelets Kam Hain

  • Jild pe neeli ya laal nishan (bruises) bina chot ke
  • Naak se khoon aana baar baar
  • Masoorhon se khoon aana
  • Choti laal dottein skin pe (petechiae)
  • Peshab ya latrine mein khoon
  • Bohat zyada period bleeding

⚠️ Agar platelets 50,000 se neeche girein toh FORI doctor se milein! 20,000 se neeche life-threatening ho sakta hai.

💊 Treatment Kya Hai?

  • Dengue mein: Zyada pani piyen, papaya leaf extract, aur rest karen
  • Severe cases mein: Platelet transfusion ki zaroorat ho sakti hai
  • Iron/B12 deficiency: Supplements aur achi ghiza
  • Regular monitoring: CBC test karwa ke platelets check karte rahein
