🤰 Pregnancy Test Kya Hai?

Pregnancy test ek simple test hai jo aapke jism mein hCG hormone (Human Chorionic Gonadotropin) check karta hai. Yeh hormone sirf pregnancy mein banta hai. Is se pata chalta hai ke aap pregnant hain ya nahi.

⏰ Pregnancy Test Kab Karein?

  • Period miss hone ke 7 din baad: Yeh sabse behtareen time hai — accuracy 99% hoti hai
  • Subah ka pehla urine: Urine test ke liye subah ka pehla sample sabse accurate hota hai (hCG concentrated hota hai)
  • Blood test jaldi detect karta hai: Blood hCG test period miss hone se pehle bhi pregnancy detect kar sakta hai (6-8 din baad conception ke)

❓ False Negative Kab Aata Hai?

  • Test bohat jaldi karna — hCG abhi enough nahi bana
  • Raat ka pani zyada pee lena — urine dilute ho jata hai
  • Expired test kit use karna

💡 Agar urine test negative aaye lekin period nahi aa raha, toh 3-5 din baad dobara test karein ya blood hCG test karwayein — yeh zyada accurate hai.
