📊 Understanding Blood Sugar Levels

Diabetes has become one of the most common diseases in the world. In Pakistan, approximately 1 in every 4 adults is at risk. That's why understanding your blood sugar levels and getting regular checkups is extremely important.

⚠️ Symptoms of Diabetes

  • Excessive thirst (polydipsia)
  • Frequent urination (polyuria)
  • Unexplained weight loss
  • Fatigue and weakness
  • Blurred vision
  • Slow wound healing
  • Tingling or numbness in hands and feet

🛡️ How to Prevent Diabetes

  • Reduce sugar intake — cut down on sweets and sugary drinks
  • Walk 30 minutes daily — regular exercise lowers blood sugar
  • Maintain a healthy weight — obesity is a major risk factor
  • Eat more vegetables and fiber — helps regulate blood sugar
  • Control portion sizes — don't overeat carbohydrates
  • Get tested every 3-6 months — early detection saves lives

💡 If diabetes runs in your family, you are at HIGH RISK. Get your blood sugar tested every 3 months!
