What is a CBC?

A Complete Blood Count (CBC) is a common blood test that evaluates your overall health and detects a wide range of disorders, including anemia, infection, and leukemia. It measures several components and features of your blood, including:

  • Red blood cells: Which carry oxygen
  • White blood cells: Which fight infection
  • Hemoglobin: The oxygen-carrying protein in red blood cells
  • Hematocrit: The proportion of red blood cells to the fluid component (plasma)
  • Platelets: Which help with blood clotting
