❓ When Should You Take a Pregnancy Test?

  • After a missed period: If your period is 7+ days late, it's time to test
  • First morning urine is best: The first urine sample of the day is the most accurate for strip tests
  • Blood tests detect earlier: A blood hCG test can detect pregnancy even before a missed period
  • When symptoms appear: Nausea, breast tenderness, fatigue, etc.
