Whether you are experiencing symptoms like extreme fatigue or just need your routine checkup, we offer a full spectrum of diabetic profiling:

  • Fasting Blood Sugar (FBS): Measures your baseline blood glucose after an 8-10 hour fasting period. Considered the gold standard for clinical diabetes screening.
  • Random Blood Sugar (RBS): Measures blood glucose levels at any time of the day, regardless of when you last ate. Highly useful for quick emergency screening.
  • HbA1c (Glycated Hemoglobin): Provides a highly accurate 3-month average of your blood sugar levels. This is the ideal test for monitoring long-term diabetes management and treatment efficacy.
  • Glucose Tolerance Test (OGTT): Specialized testing typically used during pregnancy to diagnose gestational diabetes or insulin resistance.
