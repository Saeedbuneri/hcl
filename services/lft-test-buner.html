What Does an LFT Measure?

  • ALT (SGPT): Liver enzyme — elevated levels indicate liver damage
  • AST (SGOT): Enzyme found in liver and heart tissue
  • Bilirubin: High levels cause jaundice (yellowness)
  • Alkaline Phosphatase (ALP): Bone and bile duct health
  • Total Protein & Albumin: Nutrition and liver production ability
  • GGT: Gamma-glutamyl transferase — bile duct indicator

⚠️ When Should You Get an LFT?

  • If you have jaundice (yellowish eyes/skin)

  • If you have a history of Hepatitis B or C
  • If you experience abdominal pain, fatigue, or nausea
  • For routine liver health checkup
  • If you consume fatty or processed foods regularly
