❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How should I prepare for a urine test?

No special preparation is needed. You simply provide a midstream urine sample which we collect in a clean, sterile container.

How is a urine infection detected?

The urine routine test checks for pus cells (WBC) and bacteria levels. If these are elevated, it indicates a UTI (Urinary Tract Infection).
